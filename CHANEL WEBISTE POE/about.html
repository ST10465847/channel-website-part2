Our Story

Founded in 1910 by Gabrielle “Coco” Chanel in Paris, Chanel revolutionized women’s fashion with timeless elegance, simplicity, and innovation.

Today, Chanel continues to lead in luxury fashion, accessories, fragrances, and beauty.
